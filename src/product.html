Intuitive Robotics

Native Application

Our software is specially designed to create a fluid experience with the hardware. This is why we are building it and not using retrofitted software offered by other companies. We want to have full control of all aspects of the products to guarantee that when your life depends on it the product will not fail.

Application Development

We use innovative Javascript libraries such as React and Node to develop our software, while using Ardunio to control the robotics systems.

iOS & Android

Designing the user experience to work on the two most common mobile operating systems in use decrease the learning time. This allows the operator to pick up a device, including their own smartphone, and start contributing to the fight within minutes.

Other Devices

We are always expanding the devices supported because it decreases the need for already budget restricted fire departments to need to upgrade or switch their current system. We are also looking into the future of computing including wearables and augmented reality systems to give the firefighters the best equipment that humanity has to offer.
